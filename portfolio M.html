Chicken Tikka Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza base
  • 200g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala paste
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with tikka masala paste, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken until fully cooked. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Spread tomato sauce on the pizza base.
  4. Top with cooked chicken, onion, bell pepper, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden. Slice and serve.
