Roasted Goat

Ingredients

  • 1 kg goat meat, cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Fresh rosemary (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic paste, ginger paste, and all spices.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the goat meat and let it sit for at least 1 hour.
  4. Place the goat meat on a baking tray and cover with foil.
  5. Roast for 1.5 hours, basting occasionally with its juices.
  6. Remove foil and roast for another 20-30 minutes until the meat is browned.
  7. Serve hot with a side of vegetables or rice.
